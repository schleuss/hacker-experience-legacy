The Hacked Database is the place where you can manage every IP address you currently have access to. Additionally, it will also list every bank account you cracked.

It is also the page where you can manage your installed virus, collect money from them and launch a DDoS attack.

====== Collecting Money ======
In order to collect money, you need a [[en:softwares#virus_collector|Virus Collector]].

Each virus must work at least 10 minutes and generate a minimum amount of $1. If these two conditions are not met, the virus is ignored.
